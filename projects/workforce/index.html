Workforce Productivity & Wellbeing Analytics

Tableau case study analyzing relationships between work hours, income inequality, productivity, and mental health indicators using multi-source global data and executive-ready visual storytelling.



Tableau Packaged Workbook Back to Projects

Executive Summary

Built an interactive Tableau dashboard to support comparative workforce insights across countries. The solution consolidates indicators related to work-life balance, inequality, and economic performance to help stakeholders identify patterns associated with productivity and mental health outcomes.

Dashboard Previews

Selected views highlighting global wellbeing patterns and inequality vs mental health relationships.

Key Insights

  • Observed a strong relationship between income inequality and mental health indicators across countries.
  • Highlighted regional differences in wellbeing and work-life balance patterns using geographic benchmarking.
  • Enabled trend-based exploration to compare productivity-related indicators alongside wellbeing outcomes.
